How it works

While Amazon S3 is great for storing static assets and large files, their prices are not optimized for smaller, more frequent updates. Frugality provides a cost-efficient layer on top of S3 which optimizes frequent high-volume writes, often reducing dollars to fractions of a penny.

Frugality solves these issues by batching your writes, so instead of sending 100,000 tiny objects to S3, there's only one, dramatically reducing long-term expenses and avoiding minimum size requirements.

Who's it for

Companies maintaining large object databases can migrate to Frugality, achieving better durability guarantees while saving thousands of dollars every month.
